What you will be asked to do

  1. Mouse task

    2. In this task, you will click buttons to select the right answer. Each test contains a written color word painted with an ink that does not necessarily match the word. The image below shows two examples with different cues. After doing the task for 5 minutes, your performance will be evaluated. Notice that the buttons' position might change.

  2. Writing task

    3. In this task, you will be shown two paintings and will be asked to write about them. Each picture will be visible for 5 minutes and you must use the whole time alloted to describe them. You can describe the elements of the picture, such as who is in it, how are their clothes, what they are doing, and are also encouraged to come up with a story about that picture. Some writing tasks will contain arithmetic questions. Try your best to get most of them right! An example of a painting and a composition is shown below:

  3. Questionnaires

    4. This task contains several multiple-choice questions.

  4. Watch relaxing videos

    5. In this task, feel free to adjust the audio to a comfortable volume.

The whole session should take no longer than 40 minutes. The sorting and writing tasks will be performed twice.
